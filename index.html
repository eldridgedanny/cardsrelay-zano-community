A trillion-dollar market, underserved by crypto

The global gift card market is worth over $1 trillion annually. While BTC, ETH and major stablecoins have growing global acceptance, privacy coin holders have far fewer options. For Zano holders in particular, there is almost nowhere to spend.

🌍

Massive, growing market

Gift cards are one of the most practical on-ramps to real-world spending. Brands like Amazon, Steam, Google Play, and hundreds more are redeemable globally, and now accessible with crypto through CardsRelay.

🔒

Privacy coins are underserved

BTC, ETH and major stablecoins are increasingly accepted globally. Privacy coins like Zano, Monero and Dash remain underserved, with few merchant integrations and limited real-world spending options. CardsRelay bridges that gap, enabling privacy coin holders to spend through gift cards at thousands of brands worldwide.

The Engine

Production-grade infrastructure, not a wrapper

CardsRelay is built on a custom fulfilment engine designed for reliability, automation, and rapid expansion. Adding a new payment processor or card supplier is a near-instant integration; the architecture accommodates new providers in hours, not weeks.

Integrations: Built & In Testing

CoinRemitter (ZANO)

Dedicated payment processor for ZANO. Webhook-driven payment detection with cryptographic signature verification. The exclusive home of ZANO payments on CardsRelay.

NowPayments (200+ coins)

Handles all non-ZANO cryptocurrency payments. IPN webhook integration with HMAC-SHA512 verification. Real-time payment status updates across the full coin catalogue.

Reloadly, joint launch partner

Gift card inventory partner from day one. 14,000+ products across 300+ brands in 170+ countries. OAuth2 authenticated. Orders placed and codes retrieved in under two seconds.

Tillo, joint launch partner

Second gift card inventory partner, live from launch alongside Reloadly. Provides additional brand coverage and supplier redundancy, with no single point of failure on card delivery.

Operational Features

Email verification at checkout

First-time buyers verify their email before checkout via a magic-link flow. HMAC-signed persistent cookie means returning customers are recognised automatically, with zero friction on repeat purchases.

Self-serve order tracking

Customers track their order in real time: payment confirmation, fulfilment status, and gift code retrieval. Email-gated for security. Designed to reduce support contact to near zero.

Automated payment recovery

Expired and partial payments trigger automated recovery emails with tailored next steps. Webhook-driven for NowPayments; daily reconciliation cron for CoinRemitter. No manual chase required.

Margin management system

Per-product, per-coin, per-region margin control. Service fees can be layered onto low-margin cards; enhanced discounts applied to high-margin products or for preferred payment methods like ZANO. Fully configurable without code changes.

Security

All payment webhooks are verified using cryptographic signatures before any order action is taken. Blockchain confirmation monitoring ensures payments are settled on-chain before fulfilment begins. Duplicate fulfilment protection prevents any gift code from being issued twice. Order state validation gates every fulfilment step, and transaction integrity is maintained end-to-end from payment receipt to code delivery.

Plug-and-play expansion

The integration layer is deliberately modular. A new payment processor or card supplier can be fully connected in hours, not weeks, because the engine's core does not need to change. Only a new adapter is required. This means every integration that comes after launch is faster and cheaper than the one before it. The architecture is built to scale outward, not to be rebuilt.

Ops Console

A dedicated internal operations dashboard gives full visibility over every order, brand, and support case. Order diagnosis is automated: the console surfaces the issue and recommended next step for every open order, removing manual triage entirely. The same attention to detail as the customer-facing product.

Commercials

Simple model. Clear margin. Intelligent pricing.

CardsRelay earns the spread between the wholesale cost of gift cards and the price charged to customers. Discount rates vary by brand and region, and the platform's built-in margin management system optimises pricing per product, applying service fees where margins are thin and enhanced discounts where margins are strong. Average gross margin across the catalogue is approximately 4% per transaction.
